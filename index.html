✝ Confession Guide

Preparation for Holy Confession
Before you begin: Find a quiet moment. Light a candle if you can. Read the Prayer Before Confession on the Prayer tab. Then go through each section honestly — not to count sins, but to open your heart before God. Check what applies to you. Bring this list to your priest, or simply speak from the heart. Remember: "God does not demand the impossible — He asks us to do what we can, and to desire what we cannot yet do." — St. Augustine
Prayer after examination:

O Lord my God, I confess that I have sinned against Thee in thought, word, and deed. I have not loved Thee with my whole heart. I have not loved my neighbour as myself. Have mercy on me, O Lord, in Thy loving kindness. Cleanse me from my sin, and help me to amend my life according to Thy holy will. Through Jesus Christ my Lord. Amen.
